FEATURED SUCCESS STORY

From Combat to Commerce

Captain Sarah Johnson served 8 years in Army Cyber Command before joining Watchtower's 2022 cohort with an idea for an AI-powered cybersecurity platform.

Through our program, she refined her product, connected with key investors, and built a team of talented engineers. Today, ShieldTech Systems protects critical infrastructure for 50+ government agencies and has raised $15M in venture capital.

"Watchtower didn't just give me resources—they gave me a network of people who believed in my vision and helped me navigate the transition from military officer to tech CEO," Sarah says.

"The skills I learned in the military—leadership, resilience, strategic thinking—are the same skills that make great entrepreneurs. Watchtower helped me translate that experience into business success."
